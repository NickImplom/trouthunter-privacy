Overview

TroutHunter is a fishing conditions app for Michigan rivers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

TroutHunter does not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No email addresses or contact information collected
  • No location tracking or GPS data collection
  • No user behavior tracking or analytics
  • No personal data transmitted to our servers

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite rivers list
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Data Sources

TroutHunter displays publicly available data from:

  • USGS (United States Geological Survey) - River water conditions, flow rates, and temperature data
  • NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) - Weather conditions and forecasts

We access this data through their public APIs. No personal information is shared with these services.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access to:

  • Fetch current river conditions from USGS
  • Retrieve weather data from NOAA
  • Display up-to-date fishing conditions

No Advertising or Tracking

TroutHunter does not contain:

  • Third-party advertising
  • Analytics or tracking software
  • Social media integrations
  • Marketing or promotional tracking

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information at risk. All data requests to USGS and NOAA are made over secure HTTPS connections.

Children's Privacy

TroutHunter does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in fishing conditions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date.

Contact Information

Email: trouthunterusa@gmail.com

Michigan Fishing Information

TroutHunter provides fishing condition information for educational and recreational purposes. Always follow Michigan DNR fishing regulations and obtain proper licenses.
