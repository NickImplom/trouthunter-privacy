TroutHunter Fishing App

TroutHunter is your all-in-one companion for trout and salmon fishing. Whether you're targeting brown trout in Michigan streams or chasing salmon runs, our app provides the real-time tools you need to succeed.

Key Features:

  • Live river flow, gage height, and water temperature
  • Weather forecasts and rainfall near rivers
  • Sunrise, sunset, dawn, and dusk info
  • Log catches, save fishing spots, and track success
